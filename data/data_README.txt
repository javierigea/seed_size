#######DATA README for Igea et al#######################################
###Data Readme for  "Seed size and its rate of evolution correlate with species diversification
###across angiosperms" by Javier Igea, Eleanor F Miller, Alexander ST Papadopulos, Andrew J Tanentzap
##############################################################################
1) AllKewdata.txt has the Seed Mass Measurements in the Kew Seed Information Database (downloaded
14/01/16) http://data.kew.org/sid/ Royal Botanic Gardens Kew. (2017) Seed Information Database
(SID). Version 7.1. Available from: http://data.kew.org/sid/ (June 2017)

2) genome_size_allKew.txt has the C-value measurements in the Kew Plant DNA C-values database
(downloaded 22/01/16) http://data.kew.org/cvalues/ Bennett MD, Leitch IJ. 2012. Angiosperm DNA
C-values database (release 8.0, Dec. 2012) http://www.kew.org/cvalues/

3) annual_CvalueDB.txt and perennial_CvalueDB.txt have the information on Cvalues for annual and
perennial species (downloaded 21/12/15) http://data.kew.org/cvalues/ Bennett MD, Leitch IJ. 2012.
Angiosperm DNA C-values database (release 8.0, Dec. 2012) http://www.kew.org/cvalues/

4) GlobalWoodinessDatabase.csv can be downloaded at
http://datadryad.org/resource/doi:10.5061/dryad.63q27.2/2.1
